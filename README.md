**ArtConnect AI**
AI-Powered Storytelling and Digital Exposure for Local Artisans

Problem Statement
  Local artisans often struggle to showcase their craft and stories beyond their immediate community due to limited digital literacy, lack of marketing knowledge, and absence of storytelling platforms. Traditional e-commerce focuses on sales but fails to preserve the cultural heritage and narratives behind these crafts.

Objective
  To create an AI-driven platform that empowers artisans to:
  Digitally present their craft through stories, audio, and multimedia.
  Reach new audiences globally with multilingual support.
  Preserve cultural traditions while making them accessible to modern digital audiences.

Solution Overview
  ArtConnect AI is a platform that allows artisans to upload images and descriptions of their craft. AI then:
  Generates compelling stories about the craft.
  Converts stories into audio narrations using multilingual Text-to-Speech.
  Enhances cultural context with AI insights and suggestions.
  Allows users to explore crafts via an interactive gallery, listen to artisan stories, and share them on digital platforms.
  This solution focuses on storytelling and cultural preservation, not just selling products.

Key Features
  Artisan Dashboard: Upload craft images and description, generate AI stories and narrations.
  User Explorer: Browse crafts, view AI-generated stories, listen to audio versions.
  AI-Powered Storytelling: Uses Google Cloud AI tools to create meaningful, multilingual stories.
  Craft Evolution (Future): Visualize how crafts might evolve or blend with other cultural styles.
  Virtual Heritage Preservation (Future): Archive endangered crafts digitally for cultural study.

Tech Stack
Frontend
  React.js / HTML, CSS, JS
Backend
  Flask (Python) for API endpoints
  MongoDB for storing artisan data and outputs
AI Tools (Google Cloud)
  Vertex AI: Story generation
  Vision AI: Craft image tagging (optional for prototype)
  Translation API: Multilingual content
  Text-to-Speech API: Audio narration of stories

Workflow

Artisan Input
Uploads image and description.
AI Processing
Vision AI: Extracts tags (optional).
Text AI: Generates story.
Translation: Provides multilingual text.
Text-to-Speech: Narration audio.
Content Generation
Story snippet, audio narration, and hashtags for sharing.

User Engagement
Browse crafts on a gallery or map.
Read stories, listen to narration, share content.
Analytics (Future)
Track views, likes, shares.
AI feedback for artisans on improving storytelling.
